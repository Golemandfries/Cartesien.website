English for Cambridge Primary Student Book 3PYP

The Oxford English for Cambridge Primary Student Book 3 is a book that is part of the Oxford English for Cambridge Primary series. It is published by Oxford University Press and is endorsed by Cambridge International Examinations. The book is designed to teach young learners the language and skills they need to become confident, independent students, and supports the transition to secondary school.

Primary Skills 3. Reading and Writing. Student BookPYP

Oxford Primary Skills is a series of courses designed to teach reading and writing skills to young learners. The topics and order of the units have been specially designed to complement what your students are learning in language lessons, making it the perfect partner for your current coursebook. The series has six levels, with each level including a student book, workbook, classroom presentation tool, and teacher’s pack with lesson support, curriculum design guidance, and professional development videos.
