Grade 10 Creativity, Activity, Service
CAS Guide
DescriptionThe Creativity, Activity, and Service (CAS) program is an essential component of the International Baccalaureate (IB) program1. It aims to provide students with a well-rounded education that goes beyond academic coursework by encouraging personal and social development. The CAS program requires students to complete a minimum of 150 hours of activities, including at least 50 hours in each of the three components: creativity, activity, and service. The creativity component involves activities that allow students to explore their imagination and express themselves, such as music, art, and drama. The activity component promotes physical fitness and a healthy lifestyle, such as sports, hiking, and dancing. The service component involves volunteering and community service projects that help students to develop a sense of social responsibility and awareness.
