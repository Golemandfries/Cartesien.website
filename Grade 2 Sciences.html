Grade 2 Sciences
Oxford International Primary Science Stage 2
DescriptionOxford International Primary Science is a nine-year science course that takes an enquiry-based, active-learning approach to teaching young learners the skills they need to become confident scientists. It is designed for students aged between 5 and 11 years old 1. The course is divided into 9 stages and covers a wide range of topics such as forces, light, sound, materials, and living things 1. The course focuses on scientific enquiry and global skills, and communication activities help EAL students develop fluency in scientific language.
