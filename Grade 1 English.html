Oxford English for Cambridge Primary Student Book 1
DescriptionOxford English for Cambridge Primary Student Book 1 is a part of the Oxford English for Cambridge Primary series by Oxford University Press. The series contains 6 student books, 6 workbooks, 6 teacher’s guides, and 1 Cambridge Primary Checkpoint Test Practice Book. The resources match the Cambridge curriculum.The Student Book 1 (International Primary) edition of the book has clear learning outcomes that ensure systematic development of core English skills and provide measurable targets for students and teachers. Thematic units featuring global texts give a foundation to engage and build learners’ confidence.
Oxford Primary Skills 1. Reading and Writing. Student Book
DescriptionThe Oxford Primary Skills is a 6-level course for young learners that helps develop their language skills in reading, writing, listening and speaking. The course is designed to help learners develop their language skills in a fun and engaging way. The course is divided into units that cover different topics such as animals, food, and sports. Each unit has a reading and writing section that helps learners develop their reading and writing skills. The course also includes listening and speaking activities that help learners develop their listening and speaking skills. The Student Book for Level 1 of the Oxford Primary Skills course includes 48 units that cover different topics such as animals, food, and sports. Each unit has a reading and writing section that helps learners develop their reading and writing skills. The book also includes listening and speaking activities that help learners develop their listening and speaking skills.
Oxford International English Student Activity Book 1
DescriptionOxford International English Student Activity Book 1 includes carefully levelled activities linked to develop reading comprehension, writing and speaking and listening skills. The activities are linked to the Student Anthology chapters of fiction, non-fiction and poetry from around the world to develop core language and literacy skills.
